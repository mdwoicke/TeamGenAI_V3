InteractiveGroup.ai Inc. Founders

Gerard Gooch is an American inventor, entrepreneur, and renowned tech innovator with over 25 years of experience in business transformation. He is widely recognized for his pioneering contributions to Web Application Development, Voice User Interface Design, Customer Experience (CX), and Generative AI. Gerard has held executive positions at leading companies, including Fujitsu, Tesla, NTT Ltd., Imagine Group, and Technocross, where he has consistently driven innovation and spearheaded significant growth initiatives.

Gerard's expertise in Generative AI is globally recognized, placing him within the top 0.5-1% of Generative AI Prompt and Agent Engineers worldwide, according to ChatGPT. He has led numerous AI and Generative AI projects, including consulting with Fortune 100 companies on developing new Generative AI features for an established AI solution with 50,000 users, and crafting RFPs to procure Generative AI solutions. Gerard has also created dozens of custom GPTs and specialized AI agents across various industries, such as business, healthcare, law, and education.

Some of his notable projects include an AI Assistant for US Federal and State tax preparation, multiple inventions and PoC’s including a real-time Generative AI-powered question detection and response system for meetings, and deploying over 1,800 autonomous AI (Autonomous Supervisors) for contact centers in both the financial services and insurance sectors.

Gerard is passionate about empowering the next generation through AI education. He founded YouthGPT, an initiative aimed at helping young people in America learn about and harness the power of Generative Artificial Intelligence, preparing them for a bright future heading into the AI revolution. Gerard’s commitment to making the world a better place extends beyond his work, as he is also a competitive hockey player, professional quad skater, and outdoor enthusiast.


Mike Woicke, Founder and CTO
With over a decade of experience in AI and technology, Mike has excelled in developing and deploying cutting-edge AI solutions for various industries. His expertise includes deep learning architectures, machine learning model development, and AI integration strategies, with a focus on natural language processing and multimodal chatbot development. Mike has worked with leading companies like Bayer Animal Health, Elanco, and Sharp Imaging, where he played a key role in pioneering AI proof of concept projects and significantly enhancing business operations. He is also a skilled Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) developer, specializing in advanced automations, customer journeys, and personalized messaging strategies. At Digital Response Technologies LLC, Mike designed and delivered voice-activated solutions, optimized user experiences, and developed comprehensive AI strategies, showcasing the transformative potential of AI in business. His innovative work has earned him multiple patents, including systems for omnichannel communication and patient monitoring.

Mike is passionate about exploring the latest advancements in AI and machine learning, continuously pushing the boundaries of innovation. He is also deeply committed to mentoring and contributing to open-source projects, driving the future of AI forward.
